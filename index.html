"Have you heard of "Toilet Sensor?"

Public toilet sensors is designed to help humans to accelerate their mobility in everyday life.
This sensor device will notify potential public toilet users about the availability of cubicles in the toilet.
if the sensor indicates that all the cuicles are occupied, then people will immediately look for antoher public toilet,
wihout having to enter the toilet first (public toilets in some malls must first pass through a long hallway before reaching the toilet).
